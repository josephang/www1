American Red Cross Club Mission

Red Cross Clubs, led by youth volunteers, will support the American Red Cross mission to alleviate human suffering in the face of emergencies, by being an advocate and resource for disaster preparedness education, information, and training on school campuses.

American Red Cross Club Vision

Red Cross Clubs will empower students and faculty with Red Cross knowledge and life-saving skills to best prepare their school to respond to any emergency.
